About Us

Hi! My name is Jeff Perkins and I own and Operate Ocho's Custom Creations. Ocho is my nickname I earned at work (long story) and became a bit of an alter ego I suppose. Frankly... I needed an alter ego.
In August of 2022 I found myself facing some personal struggles that came to a head. It did not take much internal reflection and I decided that I needed to end my relationship with alcohol. A visionary friend of mine
presented me with an idea, some encouragement and made it impossible for me to say no to starting a small local business. Always having an interest in my creative side and a long desire to start a small business, here we are.

Turn Any photo into a custom wall art!! Any image (and I mean ANY!) can be converted and cut. It is impossible to offend me. And YES! I can keep a secret.
